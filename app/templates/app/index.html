Aeromax Predictor

Hey there! Welcome to Aeromax Predictor. This is a web app intended to an AI model which is trained on India's air quality data to predict the air quality for the next year.

How to use?

Upload a CSV file containing the air quality data for the past 5 years and the model will predict the air quality for the next year.
